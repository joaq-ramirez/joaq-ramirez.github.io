About Me

I recently graduated CU Boulder with a Master's in Autonomous Systems and Bachelor's in Aerospace Engineering. My work experience largely deals with spacecraft design and analysis with an emphasis on controls and state estimation. Most recently I have been working on the ADCS/CDH verification and validation on a cubesat mission called SWARM-EX utilizing my embedded systems knowledge to perform acceptance tests and eventually day in the life operational tests. My interests include all things engineering, as I have a passion for building and ultimately want to use my skills to make the world a better place.

Aside from work, I mostly enjoy snowboarding in the winters and climbing/biking in the summers. Additionally, I have been breakdancing for 10 years where I taught classes for 2 years at my local dance studio in Boulder.
